About Secure Voting System

Welcome to the Secure Voting System, an advanced platform designed to make voting more secure, accessible, and efficient through cutting-edge face recognition technology.

What We Do

Our system ensures a seamless and tamper-proof voting experience by verifying voters' identities using face recognition. It eliminates the risk of voter fraud and enhances the integrity of the electoral process.

How to Use the Application

  1. Register Your Account:Provide your personal details
  2. Login to Your Account:Use your registered phone number to receive otp.
  3. Verify Your Identity:The system will use your camera to scan your face and confirm your identity. Ensure good lighting and a clear view of your face..
  4. Select an Election:Browse the list of available elections and choose the one you want to participate in.
  5. Cast Your Vote:Review the list of candidates, select your choice, and submit your vote.
  6. Receive Confirmation:A confirmation message will appear once your vote is successfully recorded.
